SPOTit is a search engine designed to give you a deeper look into Boston properties and their surrounding neighborhoods. Enter a desired address below to receive a property rating based on your building’s owner, the violations at the property and crime, noise and hotline complaints within a 300 yard radius.

To customize your search based on what you find most important, simply adjust the sliders.

Click the “Method” tab to find out how the scores are calculated!
