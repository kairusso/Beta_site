HOW IT WORKS

The total rating is an average score of the following categories:

  • Owner/Landlord responsible for the upkeep
  • Crime Reports
  • Violations
  • Service Requests from the Mayerâ€™s 24 Hour Hotline
  • Noise Disturbances

We created a probability distribution function of all properties in Boston by assigning values to individual reports/incidents under each of the 4 categories. These values were based on the severity of the incident while taking into account the cost of the fine associated with any violation.

E.g. if you property ranks above 74% of all other addresses in relation to crime, then your crime score will be 7.4.

All scores are displayed using a scale of 1-10, with 1 being the lowest and 10 being the highest and most satisfactory score. The scores also correlated to a color scheme with scores ranging as follows

  • 0-1.6 = dark red
  • 1.6-3.3 = red
  • 3.3-5 = orange
  • 5-6.6 = yellow
  • 6.6-8.3 = green
  • 8.3-10 = dark green

Furthermore, the violation score is a property specific score of an entire building, rather than a single apartment unit. The crime, noise and hotline scores are a reflection of incidents within a 250-meter radius of a property searched.

CUSTOM PREFERENCE FEATURE

Choosing to use the customized preference feature upon entry of the website will allow you to modify the average rating. Your ranking will be based on the weight of importance you assign to each of the 4 categories.
